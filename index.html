Uber's Vehicle Solutions program offers car rentals from third party partners to drivers in most major cities across the US, and there are a variety of vehicle options to fit your needs. Hear why rentals work for these drivers:

"I rent through the Uber program, and I feel that it helps me because I'm not depreciating the value of my own vehicle." - Melissa A., Illinois

"I would not have the ability to be driving an EV if it weren't for the rental program." - Melissa A., Illinois

“I think that it’s a great opportunity for someone that needs to earn and doesn’t have a car.” - Tammy M., Texas

Hertz has both gas and electric vehicles available to rent through the Vehicle Marketplace, with unlimited mileage, basic maintenance, and insurance included. You'll also get a $40 voucher to use to get to the rental branch for your rental reservation.

Hertz has over 250 locations, so you can make a reservation at a rental branch that's convenient for you. All you need to bring is a driver’s license and a credit or debit card and be eligible to go online with Uber.
